Final Thoughts and Reflection from GPS 1.2

  1. Semantics provide additional meaning to the information and content on the page. I used articles and sections the most, referring to the html5 documentation that helped be differenciate between the two
  2. Browsers will load the content by order of how they appear on the in the html page
